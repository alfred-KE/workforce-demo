Original-style business process maps for a fictitious value retailer, Northwind Retail, with an AI & automation improvement layer overlaid step-by-step — see which work automates, which stays human, and which control gaps close. All data is synthetic and directional.

DIRECTIONAL — not a business case. Automation-potential (Auto %) figures are the estimated share of a task's effort that shifts off a human with today's technology; they are judgement-based estimates, not measured savings, and technical potential is not the same as headcount reduction. Hardening these into € and FTE requires FTE by role/location, task volumes and cycle times, and the confirmed technology stack. All companies, roles and systems shown are fictitious.
